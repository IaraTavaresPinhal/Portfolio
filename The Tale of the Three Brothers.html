I was responsible for designing the interactive elements of the site, ensuring smooth navigation and immersive experiences through dynamic visuals and responsive interactions. By merging interactive design with accessibility features, the website became a welcoming space for those who traditionally struggle with reading and maintaining focus.

The Tale of the Three Brothers

An interactive site that invites every reader into a magical, personalized story

  • Essence Conveyed:
  • Offering a magical and inclusive experience where the world of stories becomes accessible, stimulating imagination and aiding focus.

The website was designed to be constantly interactive, allowing users to explore the story through mouse movements that act like a lantern, guiding them through the narrative. This interaction not only makes the experience more engaging but also aids users with ADHD in staying focused. I ensured the text was well-structured and easy to follow, with breaks and clear divisions, so those with dyslexia (like myself) could easily read and comprehend the content. The goal was to create an experience where users could lose themselves in the story, without the traditional barriers of reading.
